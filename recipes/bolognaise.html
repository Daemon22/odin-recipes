Spaghetti Bolognaise

Spaghetti Bolognaise for those hearty warm meals!

Ingredients

  • 250g Spaghetti
  • 500g Mince
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 1 Large Green Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Crushed Garlic
  • 1 Tsp Mixed Herbs
  • 1 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1 Tin Tomato Puree
  • 1 Tsp Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil

Method

  1. Dice Onion and Green Pepper then fry with garlic in oil until it starts turning translucent.
  2. Add mince and cook until almost cooked all the way through.
  3. Add Mixed Herbs and stir through. Then add Tomato Paste and Tomato Puree, mix well and add Sugar.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Cook Spaghetti and serve.
